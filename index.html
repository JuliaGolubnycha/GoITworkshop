Why have I chosen the IT sphere

Because it holds the key to unlocking my lifelong dream

Ever since I was a child, I gazed up at the night sky with wonder, dreaming of becoming an astronomer. The mysteries of the cosmos, the allure of exploring distant galaxies – they captivated me like nothing else. But as I grew older, I realized that the path to becoming an astronomer had shifted. It wasn't just about stargazing and uncovering the secrets of the universe; it was about mastering complex mathematics and data analysis. Yet, rather than deter me, this realization ignited a new passion within me. I saw an opportunity to merge my love for exploration with the practical skills of software development. By becoming a developer, I can create the very tools that astronomers rely on to push the boundaries of our understanding. I can write code that powers telescopes, analyzes astronomical data, and fuels the quest to unravel the mysteries of the cosmos. In the realm of technology, I see endless possibilities to contribute to humanity's exploration of the galaxy. And that is why I have chosen the IT sphere – because it allows me to pursue my childhood dream in a way I never imagined possible.
